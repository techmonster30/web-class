Chocolate Chips Cookie Recipe

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • 3/4 cup Brown sugar
  • Vanilla extract
  • Baking powder
  • 1 cup of Butter
  • 1/2 cup Granulated sugar
  • 1/2 tea spoon flaky Sea salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups kcc bittersweet
  • 1 cup walnuts or pecans

    • Baking instructions

    1. heat oven to 375^0F.
    2. stir flour with baking soda and salt, set aside
    3. in large mixing bowl beat butter with sugar, and brown sugar at medium speed untill creamy and lightened in color.
    4. add egg and vanila once at a time,mix on low speed untill incoporated
    5. Gradually blend dry mixture into creamed stir in nuts and chocolate chips.
    6. Drop by tablespoon onto ungreased cookie shits.
    7. Bake for 9 to 11 minutes or untill chocolate chips cookie are golden brown.
    8. Enjoy
